Income Tax Deductor

Write a prgram that inputs the salary of an employee from the user. It Deducts the income tax from the salary on the following basis:

  1. 20% income tax if the salary is above RS. 30000
  2. 15% income tax if the salary is between Rs. 20000 and Rs. 30000
  3. 10% income tax if the salary is below Rs.20000

The program finally displays salary, income tax and teh net salary.
